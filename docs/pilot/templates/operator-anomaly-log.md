## Severity guide

- `low`: minor issue, no meaningful effect on participant flow or data integrity
- `medium`: visible issue, recoverable without invalidating the cycle
- `high`: major disruption, possible impact on comparability or data integrity
- `critical`: cycle cannot continue safely without pause, rerun, or explicit deviation handling
